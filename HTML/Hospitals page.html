Public Hospitals in Johannesburg

Chris Hani Baragwanath Academic Hospital

Location: Soweto, Johannesburg

One of the largest hospitals in the world, offering comprehensive medical services and training to healthcare professionals.

Charlotte Maxeke Johannesburg Academic Hospital

Location: Parktown, Johannesburg

A major teaching hospital affiliated with the University of the Witwatersrand, providing specialized tertiary healthcare services.

Helen Joseph Hospital

Location: Auckland Park, Johannesburg

Offers a wide range of general and specialist medical services, including emergency care and chronic disease management.

Rahima Moosa Mother and Child Hospital

Location: Coronationville, Johannesburg

Specializes in maternity, neonatal, and pediatric care, serving communities across Gauteng province.

South Rand Hospital

Location: Rosettenville, Johannesburg South

Provides community-based healthcare services, including surgical, maternity, and outpatient departments.

Natalspruit Hospital

Location: Vosloorus, Johannesburg East

A regional public hospital delivering essential healthcare services to communities in Ekurhuleni and surrounding areas.

Edenvale Hospital

Location: Edenvale, Johannesburg East

District hospital offering primary and secondary healthcare services with a focus on community health improvement.

Tambo Memorial Hospital

Location: Boksburg, Greater Johannesburg

Provides general healthcare, maternity, and emergency medical services to residents in the eastern suburbs.
